The Raw Essence

Sauvage Elixir is an extraordinarily concentrated fragrance steeped in the emblematic freshness of Sauvage with an intoxicating heart of spices, a "tailor-made" lavender essence and a blend of rich woods forming the signature of its powerful, lavish and captivating trail.

Longevity: 12+ Hours (Eternal)
Best for: Evening & Winter.

Olfactive Notes

The top notes are Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Cardamom and Grapefruit; middle note is Lavender; base notes are Licorice, Sandalwood, Amber, Patchouli and Haitian Vetiver. Every drop is a testament to Dior's craftsmanship.
